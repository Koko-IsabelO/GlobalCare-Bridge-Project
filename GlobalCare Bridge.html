About

Our team members have made this comprehensive website dedicated to showcase
all of our key information and the project we are trying to pitch. Through
this this we hope to educate individuals on the lack of access to healthcare
on certain regions. Through detailed research and data visualization using
tools like Looker Studio or Canva, we will highlight the socio-economic
barriers that hinder the healthcare access. Finally, we will focus on
specific diseases and compare that with other countries, as well as
qualitative research to show the urgency
of the problem.












           











>>Unequal access to healthcare leads to higher rates of preventable diseases,
increased healthcare costs, and shortened life expectancy. When individuals
lack timely medical care due to socioeconomic or geographical barriers, they
often experience worsened health conditions that could have been prevented or
treated earlier, resulting in higher healthcare costs and reduced quality of
life. Ensuring universal access to healthcare is not just a matter of economic
efficiency but a fundamental human right. It's essential for promoting health
equity and social justice, enabling all individuals to receive necessary medical
treatments and preventive care, thereby improving overall public health and fostering
a more equitable society where everyone has the opportunity to live a healthy and
productive life.<<

 Our Service
Our target audience includes individuals in underserved
areas with limited healthcare access. Particularly
those from low-income or marginalized backgrounds
facing barriers like transportation challenges
and sparse local healthcare facilities. 		Many of them struggle to access timely medical care, which can
lead to untreated chronic diseased and increased financial
burden as a result of delayed diagnoses. 		In what specific circumstance might they use our
service? They might use our service when they
have a medical emergency or in need a
diagnosis when they are unable to
access it.
